Flowers

White flower

White flowers are often symbols of purity, innocence, and peace. They are commonly used in various cultural and religious ceremonies, such as weddings and funerals, to convey a sense of reverence and tranquility. Different white flowers can have unique meanings; for example, white lilies are associated with renewal and rebirth, while white roses symbolize true love and loyalty. Their simplicity and elegance make them a popular choice for bouquets, gardens, and decorations, evoking a serene and timeless beauty.
